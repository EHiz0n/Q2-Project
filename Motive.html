Motives

I think it's safe to say that the question of "why this was made" may have lingered around your mind. Well, the answer is quite simple. This website was created for the sole purpose of showing the diverse flavors and culinary culture that the Philippines has to offer to not just other Filipinos but to the whole world too. To have a culture is to share it with others, express it rather than gatekeep it and food is not excluded from this phrase of mine. Food is an integral part of cultures and to keep it away from those who are interested is something I am not fond of and I think that culinary culture should be available to everyone.
